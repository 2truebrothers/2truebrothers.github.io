Mission

Through the work in our community, we’re focusing on networking with volunteer peer counselors, building stronger roots within our community, providing opportunities and resources for higher education, and connecting our mentees with their potential.

Nurturing Empowered Youth

We’re working to create a better world for everyone. We raise awareness, provide programs, and fund initiatives that nurture and empower youth.

Programs

Our purpose is to provide educational mentoring services to aid in mental, social, and physical health through Education, Economic Empowerment, and Health & Wellness.

About

We are a non-profit, community-based organization that provides mentoring services to aid in mental, social, and physical health. Our goal is to foster relationships with local businesses and leaders to assist minority youth in obtaining gainful employment.

Our Story

Founded by brothers Andre Hines and Audrick Brown, both natives of Prince William County, Virginia, we understand the challenges youth face during critical stages of development and are dedicated to providing the necessary resources to shape a better future.

What We Do

Our program provides guidance, emotional support, and role modeling to equip mentees with career navigation skills and life resources that foster personal and professional growth.

Impact

Through respect and understanding, we emphasize education, excellence, and focus, providing resources for success while fostering positive character development.

History

Andre Hines founded 2 True Brothers to address a lack of male role models in his community. With shared experiences, Hines and his brother work tirelessly to empower youth through dedicated resources and guidance.

Vision

In a perfect world, every young adult would have access to the resources needed to become their best selves. Our initiatives aim to turn this vision into reality for youth in our community.
