Mission Statement

Our mission is to empower and uplift the youth to become the best versions of themselves.

Nurturing Empowered Youth

We’re working to make the world a better place to live for everyone. We spread awareness for our cause, offer programs and services to our community, and fund initiatives for change.

Programs

Our purpose is to provide educational mentoring services to aid in mental, social, and physical health through Education, Economic Empowerment, and Health & Wellness.

About

We are a non-profit community-based organization that specializes in providing educational mentoring services to aid in mental, social, and physical health. We foster relationships with local businesses and leaders to assist minority youth in obtaining or creating gainful employment.

“2 True Brothers was created to help the youth in our community who are looking to better themselves, and our goal is to provide them with the necessary resources.” - Andre Hines, President

Our Story

2 True Brothers was founded by brothers Andre Hines and Audrick Brown, both natives of Prince William County, Virginia. Having similar upbringings, they understand firsthand the challenges youth face during their critical stages of development and are dedicated to providing the resources needed to positively change their futures.

What We Do

Our program provides guidance, motivation, emotional support, and role modeling. We equip our mentees with the knowledge they need to navigate their careers and offer resources that promote personal and professional growth.

Impact

Through mutual respect and understanding, our mentors emphasize the importance of education, excellence, and focus. We create a nurturing community environment to foster the development of our young men.
